Frequently asked

Where is my data stored?

Entirely on your device, inside the app's sandbox. Nothing is uploaded. On macOS you'll find the local database at ~/Library/Containers/com.kitnet.task.app/Data/Library/Application Support/KinetTask/. You can back it up with Time Machine or any backup tool.

How do I delete all my data?

Delete your tasks inside the app, or simply uninstall KinetTask — that removes everything. There is no account to delete because KinetTask has no accounts.

The AI (task breakdown / related tasks) isn't doing much.

Those features work best with a 3B-or-larger local model or Apple Foundation Models (macOS 26 / iOS 26). Open Settings → AI and load a larger model. With a very small model or none loaded, KinetTask falls back to built-in rule-based parsing — it still captures tasks correctly, just with less AI reasoning.

Natural-language capture isn't parsing the date.

Try phrasing the time explicitly (e.g. "tomorrow 3pm" / "明天下午3点"). Even without a model, KinetTask's rule parser handles common dates and times; loading a model improves title and tag extraction.

Does KinetTask sync between my devices?

Not in this version. Everything stays local by design (that's the privacy promise). iCloud sync may come as an opt-in feature in a future release.
